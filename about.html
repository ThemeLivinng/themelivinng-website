Our Story

Craft. Intention.
Quiet Luxury.

Design Philosophy

We Believe Space
Is a Language.

Founded on the belief that great interiors emerge from the dialogue between a designer and a client — not from a catalogue or a trend — Theme Livinng Interiors was established to serve those who require more than decoration.

Our studio is rooted in Delhi NCR, but our work is guided by a global sensibility — informed by the great interior traditions of Europe and Japan, and grounded in the warmth and richness of Indian craft heritage.

We believe that luxury is not about opulence. It is about the intelligence of a well-considered plan, the pleasure of a perfectly chosen material, and the silence of a room that needs nothing more.

What Guides Us

Principles of
Our Practice.

Attention Without Compromise

Every detail is deliberate. From the junction of two materials to the angle of light through a window, we see what others overlook and design for it accordingly.

The Client at the Centre

Our work exists to serve you — your life, your rhythm, your aspiration. We listen before we design, and we listen again before we finalise.

Luxury and Functionality as One

A beautiful space that does not work is a failed space. We design for daily life at its finest — effortless to inhabit, extraordinary to experience.

Enduring Over Ephemeral

We do not chase trends. We pursue timelessness — creating spaces that age with grace and remain relevant for generations to come.

A Personal Word

From the Founder.
